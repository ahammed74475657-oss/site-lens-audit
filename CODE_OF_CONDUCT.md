# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, constructive and specific. Assume good intent, but take responsibility for the impact of your words and actions.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks.
- Publishing private information.
- Repeated low-effort disruption.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or block users who make the project harder to participate in.
